Death Note:

Death Note is a psychological thriller anime that explores the dark side of justice, power, and morality. The story follows Light Yagami, a brilliant student who discovers a mysterious notebook that allows him to kill anyone by writing their name in it. Believing he can create a perfect world free of crime, Light begins to act as a god-like figure, while a legendary detective known as L works to stop him. With intense mind games, smart storytelling, and constant twists, Death Note keeps viewers questioning what is right and wrong. It is an anime that goes beyond action, focusing on intelligence, strategy, and human nature, making it a timeless classic for both new viewers and long-time anime fans.
